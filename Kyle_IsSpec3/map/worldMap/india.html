Places in India

India

India is a country in South Asia. It is the seventh-largest country by area, the second-most populous country (with over 1.4 billion people), and the most populous democracy in the world.

Khajuraho Group Of Monuments In Madhya Pradesh

The Khajuraho Group of Monuments are a group of Hindu temples in Madhya Pradesh, India, about 175 kilometers southeast of Jhansi. They are one of India's most famous historical sites and are known for their erotic sculptures and architecture.

Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is an ivory-white marble mausoleum on the south bank of the Yamuna river in the Indian city of Agra. It was commissioned in 1632 by the Mughal emperor Shah Jahan to house the tomb of his favorite wife, Mumtaz Mahal; it also houses the tomb of Shah Jahan himself.

The Golden Temple In Punjab

The Golden Temple is a gurudwara located in the city of Amritsar, Punjab, India. It is the most important pilgrimage site of Sikhism. The temple is built around a man-made pool, which is sacred because it was here that the first Guru, Guru Nanak, is said to have discovered the simple message of Sikhism.

The Hampi Temple Complex In Karnataka

The Hampi Temple Complex is a group of Hindu temples located in the ruins of the city of Hampi, Karnataka, India. The temples are known for their intricate carvings and architecture.

The Konark Sun Temple In Orissa

The Konark Sun Temple is a 13th-century CE Hindu temple located in the Konark district of Orissa, India. It is one of India's most famous historical sites and is known for its massive stone chariot.

The Monolithic Gomateshwara Statue In Karnataka

The Monolithic Gomateshwara Statue is a 7th-century CE statue of the Buddha located in the Channarayapatna village of Karnataka, India. It is one of India's most famous historical sites and is known for its intricate carvings and architecture.

The Nalanda University In Bihar

The Nalanda University is an ancient university located in the Nalanda district of Bihar, India. It was one of the most famous universities in the world during the Gupta Empire and is known for its ancient library and teachings.
